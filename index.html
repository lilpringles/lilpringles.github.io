The filtered data is focused on the Fate Franchise anime adaptations which brings in a lot of money both in Japan and around the glode. In 2018, Fate/Grand Order (Gacha mobile ap) grossed $1.2 billion. After parsing through the initial dataset from Kraggle searching the title column for the string ‘Fate/’ i create a dataframe with the resuts. I noticed that the scrapped dataset from Kraggle hasnt been updated from the results since I know the studio ufotable has released 2 movies that are excluded from the dataframes. But with that I created a bar graph that has the MyAnimeList score for the various anime adaptation series and color coded by the various studios that animated the series (tv or movie). I also added a toolkit that when one hovers over a particular bar you can see the exact score recorder for that series.

In general, I was curious what fate series had the highest score and which score distribution per animation studio. There is a consensus among the community that ufotable produces the highest quality Fate adaptations and the graph supports those claims. In particular, the fate/zero adaptation which is generally regarded as the critically the best in the series ranks the highest in score. Both season 1 and season 2 of Fate/zero I used a bar mark to keep things simple since there was a learning curve with Altair that was worth the effort.
